// lore info (each of these has '-x' appended, x stands for id of a given family member -
   1 means player, 2 - father, 3 - mother, 4 - grandfather, etc.):
  
  - name
  - uuid
  - username (name + uuid)
  - height
  - weight
  - hairLength
  - age
  - birthday
  - race
  - hairColor
  - eyeColor

  - relation - name of relation, makes it easy to make dialog
    (eg i knew your {relation-3}, she had {hairColor-3} hair)
  - knownFamilySize - count of family members specified during character creation
    (eg. 3 if only parents and player are specified) (not sure if it counts in player at the moment)


// gameplay info:

  - class (warrior, wizard or archer) - defines images used for player character and it's bullet
  - level - stuff gets multiplied by it

  - health - health = endurance * 100
  - endurance - ^
  - defence - gets substracted from the damage player intercepts
  - damage - average damage = strength * 10, min damage = damage / 2, max damage = damage * 1.5 (all rounded)
  - strength - ^
  - dexterity - does nothing
  - energy - fatigue points regeneration speed = energy
